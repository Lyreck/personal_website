About Me

I am an engineering and public policy student interested in the role of algorithms in society, and how we can bridge technical and social expertise.

What is this website ?

The objective of this place is to centralize my work, passions, and thoughts. You will find my reflections on topics varying from optimization to machine learning, as well as social approaches to technology, and personal homelab experiments.

I believe in the power of open source and collaboration and wish not only to centralize things for myself, but to make this platform available to anyone whose interests might intersect with what I will publish. Have a nice time!
